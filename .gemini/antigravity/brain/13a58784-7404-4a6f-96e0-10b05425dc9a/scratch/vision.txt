When a user asks EYES a question here is what happens:The question is converted into a mathematical representation called a vectorThat vector is compared against thousands of the user's stored memoriesThe 10 most relevant memories are retrievedThose 10 memories are sent to Claude along with the questionClaude reads the memories, applies its world knowledge, and generates an answerClaude API costs roughly €1 per user per month at normal usage. EYES gets harder to leave the longer someone uses it.Month 1 — 1,000 memories indexed.
Month 6 — 6,000 memories indexed including everything added since they joined.
Month 12 — their entire digital life is in there.

Leaving EYES means losing all of that. The switching cost grows every single month.

This is called a data flywheel retention model. The product literally becomes harder to leave over time.We also build four specific retention mechanisms into the product from day one:Weekly Memory DigestEvery Monday, EYES sends an email: "Here is what you said this week in 2021." One paragraph. One link back to the app. Users open it every week because it is about them. That email has the highest open rate of anything we will ever send.Memory Anniversary"Three years ago today you wrote this." Like Facebook Memories but from your own indexed history across every platform. The emotional hook is undeniable. Nobody deletes an app that makes them feel something about their own life.Reputation ScoreA single number that tracks their public digital reputation over time. Updated monthly. Gamified.
